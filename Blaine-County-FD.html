BLAINE COUNTY FIRE RESCUE

Since 1900, the Blaine County Fire Rescue has been doing their best to protect the citizens of Los Santos and Blaine County. The mission of BCFR is to provide protection of life, property, and the environment from the effects of fires, medical emergencies and all other hazards, through the use of whatever apparatus possible to preserve life.

GET INVOLVED TODAY

From Minor Medicals Calls and Motor Vehicle Accidents to Hazardous Materials Incidents and Structure Fires, and even Major Wildland Fires, The Blaine County Fire Rescue, San Andreas Lifeline, and the San Andreas Forestry Service are always ready to protect every civilian in any situation we face.
